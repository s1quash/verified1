## Prop Firm Trade Copier Rules: What is Allowed?

Many algo-traders use trade copiers to manage multiple funded accounts. However, prop firms are very strict about how you use them.

### ✅ WHAT IS ALLOWED (Personal Copying):
*   Copying trades from your own personal retail broker account to your prop firm account.
*   Copying trades from one prop firm account you own to another prop firm account you own.
*   Using software like **Social Trader Tools** or **FXBlue** to manage your own master account.

### ❌ WHAT IS BANNED (Third-Party Copying):
*   Copying trades from a public signal provider or Telegram group.
*   Purchasing an "Account Management" service where someone else trades for you.
