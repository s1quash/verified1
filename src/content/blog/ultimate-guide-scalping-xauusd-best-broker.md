## 📈 A Proven 5-Minute XAUUSD Scalping Strategy

To scalp gold effectively, you need a strategy based on momentum and volume, not just lagging indicators. Here is a highly effective 5-Minute Breakout Strategy:

### The Setup:
*   **Timeframe:** 5-Minute Chart (M5).
*   **Indicators:** 9 EMA (Exponential Moving Average), 21 EMA, and VWAP (Volume Weighted Average Price).

### The Rules (For a Buy/Long Setup):
1.  **Wait for the Overlap:** Only look for setups during the London/NY overlap session.
2.  **Identify the Trend:** Price must be trading above the VWAP, and the 9 EMA must cross above the 21 EMA.
3.  **The Pullback:** Wait for a sharp 1 to 2-minute pullback that touches the 9 EMA.
4.  **The Entry:** As soon as a 5-minute bullish candle closes rejecting the 9 EMA, enter a Buy order.
5.  **Risk Management:** Place a strict Stop-Loss 15 pips below the entry candle. Set your Take-Profit at 20 to 30 pips.

Because gold moves so fast, this trade will usually hit your Take-Profit or Stop-Loss within 10 to 15 minutes.
