## Why Do Offshore Prop Firms Deny Payouts? (The B-Book Trap)

To understand what makes a prop firm "legit," you must understand how fraudulent firms operate. Most unverified, standalone prop firms operate a **B-Book model**. This means your trades are never sent to a live market; they are kept on a simulated server. Therefore, the prop firm is the direct counterparty to your trades.

*   When you fail a challenge, the firm keeps your fee (**Profit**).
*   When you pass and request a payout, the firm pays you out of its own pocket (**Loss**).

This creates a massive conflict of interest. To avoid bankruptcy, they resort to manufactured payout denials—inventing hidden drawdown rules, banning your trading style, or delaying your payout for months.

## The Solution: Broker-Backed Prop Firms

A broker-backed prop firm eliminates this conflict of interest. These firms partner with (or are built by) established, multi-million dollar retail brokerages.

Because they have access to genuine institutional liquidity pools and **A-Book (Straight-Through Processing) execution**, your payout is just a tiny fraction of the broker's daily turnover. They don't need you to fail to survive. In fact, if you are a consistently profitable trader, the backing broker can copy your trades into the live market, meaning they make money alongside you.
